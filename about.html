About Us

Sonnex Surveyors is an independent firm of surveyors based in Sevenoaks, Kent, offering a range of residential surveying and valuation services in Kent and the surrounding counties. Richard Sonnex MRICS became a Chartered Building Surveyor in XXXX. Having spent a number of years in Sevenoaks Local Authority surveying departments he left to work for a well-established private surveying Practice in Sevenoaks. In 2010 he established Sonnex Surveyors, chartered building surveyors and construction consultants.

In addition to residential survey work incorporating full building surveys, Homebuyer reports and restricted surveys/specific defect reports; we are able to provide advice concerning Listed buildings, valuations for probate, insurance and market purposes; and advice to businesses at the start and end of lease agreements such as condition schedules and dilapidations claims. We also advise in the case of Party Wall matters.

Sonnex Surveyors

Richard Sonnex

Managing Director
BSc (Hons) MRICS

Richard is a chartered building surveyor who qualified in 1995. He now manages the business, and also undertakes professional work, particularly (but not exclusively) dilapidations, party walls and building surveys.

Since 2003 Richard has been an assessor for the RICS Assessment of Professional Competence (APC), as well as advising and mentoring APC candidates working towards chartered status. He is a member of the Pyramus and Thisbe Club which promotes excellence in party wall practice.

In his spare time Richard watches and occasionally plays sport including football & golf.
