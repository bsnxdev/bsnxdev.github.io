Building Contracts & Projects Management

Sonnex Surveyors can design and manage building schemes of virtually any size, whether it is a simple alteration through to a major development. We offer a full project management service for residential and commercial construction projects, which extends from inception to completion of the building works.

We can capably deliver a project from Planning/Building Regulations stage right through to Final Completion.

Our Project Co-Ordination Services include the following:

  • Preparation of detailed Specification of Works (with approximate quantities)
  • Tender Procedure and Analysis of returned tenders
  • Party Wall advice
  • Preparation of JCT Contract Documentation
  • Pre-Contract Meeting with successful Contractor
  • Monitoring progress and quality of work by periodic site visits
  • Administering terms of Building Contract, including regular Site Meetings
  • Preparing regular Cost Schedule information
  • Issuing Valuation Certificates (based upon actual value of work completed)
  • Agreeing Final Account with Building Contractor
  • Preparing ‘Snagging Lists’ of unfinished items at Practical Completion and at End of Rectification Period.

Exact requirements will be tailored to Clients’ individual needs. To discuss your project requirements contact our office.
