Land & Property Development Opportunities

We specialise in identifying green and brown field sites with good investment potential in the Sevenoaks and Kent area. We also have an established network of reputable partners in both development and investment facets allowing us to act as facilitators for high ROI schemes.

Residential Development

We can advise our clients on their residential development opportunities, whether they are public or private, housebuilder or developer.

One approach we can provide is to create value through the planning and development process. We can promote clients' land through the planning system and deal with site-specific planning applications. We can also advise landowners on options, promotion agreements and joint ventures. Our thorough knowledge of the local area means we are able to advise on our clients' landholdings to achieve the best price on sale.

Development Facilitation

We are constantly receiving and acquiring updates regarding potential sites within our catchment area. Coupled with our lengthy experience of working with partners in our locality we are able to connect the correct developers with the right investors and vice versa.

We actively encourage developers and investors to contact us speculatively to enquire about opportunities we know of in the area.

For property development advice from a local area expert contact our office to discuss opportunities.
