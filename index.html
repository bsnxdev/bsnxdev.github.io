Building Surveys

We understand that the purchase of property, both residential and commercial, comprise significant investments for our clients.

As such we can provide you with comprehensive professional residential and commercial building surveys, identifying any pitfalls before they arise and helping you both protect and realise the value of your real estate assets.

Listed Building Specialists
(Grade II, Grade II* & Grade I)

Sonnex Surveying specialises in building survey inspections, alterations and extensions to all listed buildings.

Our client base has extended from the converse upgrading and extension of grade II listed hall houses to grade II* & grade I school and church buildings.

Architectural Design Services

We offer an array of design and construction services, for properties ranging from Listed buildings through to modern structures.

These include extensions and conversions of existing buildings and new builds requiring either demolition of existing structures or green field sites.
